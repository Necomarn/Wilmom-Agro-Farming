About WAF Services

WILMO AGRO FARMING (WAF) Services is a small business in Wewak, East Sepik Province, Papua New Guinea. We specialize in providing high-quality cocoa root stalkers, transport and delivery services, and expert support for local farmers.

Cocoa Root Stalkers

Transport and Delivery
